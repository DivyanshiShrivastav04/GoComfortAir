Professional Duct Cleaning Services

Comprehensive air duct cleaning to improve indoor air quality, system efficiency, and your family's health. Advanced equipment and certified technicians.

Why Choose Professional Duct Cleaning?

Your home's air ducts circulate air throughout your living space multiple times per day. Over time, these ducts accumulate dust, debris, allergens, and even mold, which can significantly impact your indoor air quality and HVAC system efficiency. Our professional duct cleaning services remove these contaminants, improving both air quality and system performance.

Signs You Need Duct Cleaning

  • Visible dust buildup around vents and registers
  • Musty or stale odors from the ventilation system
  • Increased allergy symptoms or respiratory issues
  • Recent home renovation or construction
  • Pest infestation in ductwork
  • Visible mold growth in or around ducts
  • Inconsistent airflow between rooms
  • Higher than usual energy bills
  • HVAC system running more frequently

Our Duct Cleaning Process

We use industry-leading equipment and follow NADCA (National Air Duct Cleaners Association) standards for thorough, effective cleaning:

  • Complete system inspection with camera technology
  • Containment setup to prevent dust spread
  • Powerful vacuum collection system setup
  • Mechanical agitation to loosen debris
  • Thorough cleaning of supply and return ducts
  • Register and grille cleaning and sanitizing
  • Optional antimicrobial treatment application
  • Final inspection and system testing
  • Before and after photos documentation

What We Clean

  • Supply air ducts and return air ducts
  • Registers, grilles, and diffusers
  • Heat exchangers and cooling coils
  • Condensate drain pans and drip pans
  • Fan motor and fan housing
  • Air plenum and air handling unit housing
  • Air filter and filter housing

Health and Efficiency Benefits

Professional duct cleaning provides multiple benefits for your home and family:

  • Improved indoor air quality and reduced allergens
  • Enhanced HVAC system efficiency and airflow
  • Reduced energy costs and utility bills
  • Extended HVAC equipment life
  • Elimination of unpleasant odors
  • Reduced dust throughout the home
  • Better respiratory health for family members

When to Schedule Duct Cleaning

The EPA and NADCA recommend having your ducts professionally cleaned every 3-5 years for most homes. However, certain conditions may require more frequent cleaning, such as pets, smokers, recent renovations, or family members with allergies or respiratory conditions.
