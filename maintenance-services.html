Preventive HVAC Maintenance

Regular maintenance is the key to keeping your HVAC system running efficiently and preventing costly breakdowns. Our comprehensive maintenance services include thorough inspections, cleaning, adjustments, and minor repairs to ensure optimal performance year-round. Preventive maintenance can extend your system's life by years while reducing energy costs.

Our Maintenance Services Include

  • Complete system inspection and performance testing
  • Cleaning of coils, filters, and system components
  • Lubrication of moving parts and motors
  • Thermostat calibration and testing
  • Electrical connection inspection and tightening
  • Refrigerant level check and adjustment
  • Ductwork inspection for leaks and damage
  • Safety control testing and verification
  • Indoor air quality assessment
  • Energy efficiency optimization

Seasonal Maintenance

We recommend scheduling maintenance twice yearly - once before the heating season and once before the cooling season. This ensures your system is ready for peak demand periods and operating at maximum efficiency when you need it most.
