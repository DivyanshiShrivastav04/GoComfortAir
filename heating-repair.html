Heating Repair Services

Professional heating system repairs for furnaces, heat pumps, and boilers. Keep your home warm and comfortable with our expert service.

Expert Heating System Repairs

When your heating system fails, especially during cold weather, you need immediate professional help. Our certified heating technicians specialize in all types of heating system repairs, from gas furnaces to electric heat pumps and boiler systems. We provide fast, reliable service to restore warmth to your home or business.

Heating Systems We Service

  • Gas furnaces (all makes and models)
  • Electric furnaces and heating systems
  • Heat pumps (air-source and geothermal)
  • Boiler systems and radiant heating
  • Ductless mini-split heat pumps
  • Packaged heating units
  • Hybrid heating systems
  • Oil-fired heating systems

Common Heating Problems We Fix

  • Furnace not producing heat or insufficient heating
  • System won't start or ignition problems
  • Strange noises, rattling, or banging sounds
  • Frequent cycling or short cycling issues
  • Thermostat not responding or inaccurate readings
  • Poor airflow or uneven heating
  • High energy bills and inefficient operation
  • Gas leaks or safety concerns
  • Heat pump not switching between modes
  • Pilot light or igniter failures

Our Heating Repair Process

We follow a comprehensive diagnostic approach to identify and fix heating problems efficiently:

  • Complete system inspection and safety check
  • Advanced diagnostic testing of all components
  • Clear explanation of problems and repair options
  • Transparent, upfront pricing with no surprises
  • Professional repair using OEM quality parts
  • Thorough system testing and safety verification
  • Comprehensive warranty on all work performed

Emergency Heating Service

Heating emergencies don't wait for business hours. When your heating system fails during cold weather, we're available 24/7 to provide emergency repair service. Our emergency technicians carry fully stocked service vehicles and can handle most repairs on the spot.

We prioritize safety in all our heating repairs. Gas-fired systems receive special attention to ensure safe operation and compliance with local codes. Our technicians are trained in the latest safety protocols and carry gas leak detection equipment.
