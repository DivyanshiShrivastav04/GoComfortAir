24/7 Emergency HVAC Services

When your heating or cooling system fails, we're here to help. Fast, reliable emergency HVAC repairs available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

When You Need Emergency HVAC Service

HVAC emergencies don't wait for convenient times. Whether it's a sweltering summer day when your AC fails or a freezing winter night when your heater stops working, you need immediate professional help. Our emergency service technicians are available 24/7, including weekends and holidays, to restore comfort to your home or business.

What Qualifies as an HVAC Emergency?

  • Complete system failure during extreme weather
  • No heat during freezing temperatures
  • No cooling during dangerous heat conditions
  • Gas leaks or suspected gas line problems
  • Electrical issues with burning smells
  • Water leaks from HVAC equipment
  • Carbon monoxide detector activation
  • Unusual noises indicating potential damage
  • Frozen pipes from heating system failure
  • Commercial HVAC failures affecting business operations

Emergency Service Process

When you call our emergency line, here's what happens:

  • Immediate response from our emergency dispatch team
  • Quick assessment of your emergency situation
  • Dispatch of nearest available certified technician
  • Real-time updates on technician arrival time
  • Rapid diagnosis and emergency repair plan
  • Temporary solutions if permanent repair requires ordering parts
  • Complete repair with quality parts and workmanship
  • Follow-up service to ensure continued operation

Emergency Safety Measures

While waiting for our technician to arrive, follow these safety guidelines:

  • If you smell gas, evacuate immediately and call the gas company
  • Turn off the system if you notice burning smells or smoke
  • Never attempt to repair gas lines or electrical components
  • Keep the area around HVAC equipment clear
  • If pipes are frozen, turn off water supply to prevent bursting
  • Use alternative heating/cooling safely while waiting for service

Preventing HVAC Emergencies

While we're always ready to help with emergencies, regular maintenance can prevent many emergency situations. Consider our maintenance service plans to keep your system running reliably and catch potential problems before they become emergencies.
